Types of Stingrays

While stingrays live in all, temperate, costal, and subtropical waters, they are at risk of endangerment. There are around 500 species of stingrays, and each one plays a part in keeping oceanic food chains flowing. There are eight stingray families, such as Hexatrygonidas, Plesiobatidae, Urolophidae, Urotrygonidae, Dasyatidae, Potamotrygonidae, Gymnuridae, and Myliobatidae.
